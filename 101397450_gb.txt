Information about George Brown College

George Brown College of Applied arts and technology is a public, fully aaccredited college of applied arts and technology with three campuses in downtown Toronto Ontario, Canada. Established in 1967
It is named after the nineteenth century politician and newspaper publisher, George Brown
The college offers over 150 full time programs in different fields, such as: arts, business, construction, health, hospitality, technology and culinary arts.
The programs can be diploma programs, advanced diplomas, degree programs, graduate and certificate programs for full time opportunities.
The school has a majority of full time students, a good percentage of international students and a smaller one for part time students
